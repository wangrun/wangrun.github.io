Research Interests

     My research interests are at the intersection of computer security and AI. Recently, my primary research focus on Software and Systems Security, and AI Security.

  • Software and Systems Security. I am interested in using AI techniques to address software and systems security problems (e.g. malware detection, vulnerability analysis, binary analysis, etc) in Android and IoT platforms.

  • AI Security. My research aims at building more robust, safe, and interpretable AI systems by performing the certification (e.g. testing and verification) and explanation of AI systems.
